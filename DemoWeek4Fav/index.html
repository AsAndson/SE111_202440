All about Persona 3 Reload

A brief summary of the game Persona 3 Reload

Persona 3 Reload is a remake of an old game called Persona 3. It was relased in 2024 with new voice actors and new graphics. The game starts off with a teenager going to a new school in japen. The student gets attcked after three days of beimg in the new place by these things called shadows. When he is attacked he awakens a power that was stored inside of him called a persona. After he awakens his power he joined up with some other highschool students to fight off more shadows to save the world from getting dystoried.
